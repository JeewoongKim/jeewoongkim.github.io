## Research Interest

Jeewoong researches dynamic program analysis for software verification and debugging.  
His work focuses on *test case generation* for hard-to-test program behaviors and *doc-to-test traceability* :
  - Greybox Fuzzing
  - Continuous Testing
  - Automated Debugging
